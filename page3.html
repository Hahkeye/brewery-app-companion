Flip or Sip

Rules

All you need for this game is a coin, an opponent, and shots of your favorite alcohol. Start by deciding who's going to flip the coin first. The other person must guess what side, heads or tails, the coin landed on. If he or she gets it correct, it's his or her turn to flip the coin. If the person guesses incorrectly, you flip the coin again. If he or she guesses correctly this second time, the person must answer any question you have for them.

Speed Facts

Rules

I hope you know your opponent well for this game! The idea of this game is to go back and forth with your opponent stating facts about each other that you know. If a fact is incorrect or the person takes more than three seconds to come up with a fact, he or she gets a strike. When a person gets three strikes, they must take a shot. Continue playing until neither of you can think of any more facts.

Truth or Dare

Rules

Putting a spin on the normal version of Truth or Dare, one person picks truth or dare. If he or she picks the truth, but does not want to answer the question asked, the person must drink. If he or she chooses to dare and does not want to complete the task, the person must drink. Take turns doing this. The game will get crazier the drunker the players get.
